Kettlebell Workout Tips

Simple habits that make training safer and more effective.

Tips

  • Learn the hip hinge before swinging heavy
  • Keep your core braced on presses and cleans
  • Use chalk if your grip slips
  • Start with volume before loading

Consistency beats complexity. Practice the basics and progress gradually.
