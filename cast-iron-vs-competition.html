Cast Iron vs Competition

Which kettlebell type actually fits your training?

TypeBest ForNotes
Cast IronHome gym, general strengthCheaper, rugged, wider availability
CompetitionSerious kettlebell sportUniform size, precise technique

Bottom Line

Most buyers should choose cast iron. Competition bells are ideal if you train heavily with complex kettlebell movements.
