Kettlebell Buying Guide

Choose the right bell for swings, presses, and home training.

Start With the Right Weight

Beginners should start lighter than they think. Technique matters more than loading the bell.

  • Men: 16 kg is the common starting point
  • Women: 8–12 kg is common
  • Buy two bells if you can — a light and a heavy one

Cast Iron vs Coated

Cast iron is the classic choice. Vinyl and powder coat protect floors and improve comfort, which is great for home use.

What to Avoid

  • Wobbly handles
  • Poorly finished seams
  • Unknown brands with no reviews
